Restaurants

You can't beat Gunwharf Quays for a selection of restaurants and food, located just a few minutes walk from the dockyard. Gunwharf Quays shopping centre has a range of restaurants to suit all tastes including Azzurro, Cafe Rouge, Chiquito, Frankie & Benny's, Indian Palace, Jamie's Italian, La Tasca, Loch Fyne, Nandos, Pizza Express, Wagamama and Water Margin.
